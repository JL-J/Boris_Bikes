User Story

| Nouns          |       Verbs |
|----------------|-------------|
|   Person       |    Use      |
|   Bike         | Release     |
|Docking station |    See      |


### Person ###
- check to see if the bike is working
- choose the working bike  
- uses the bike

### Bike ###
- checks and returns its own working status

### Docking station ###
- releases the bike
